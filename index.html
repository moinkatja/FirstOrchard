My first orchard game

Try to pick the fruits from the trees before the raven reaches the end of the path. If the player succeeds in harvesting all the fruits and has it all in the basket before the raven has reached the orchard, he/she wins.
If the raven passes the last path card and stands in the orchard before all the fruits have been picked, the player loses against the quick thief.
Good luck!
    On the dice appears:
  • One of the fruits: apple, cherry, pear or plum. A fruit will be picked and placed into the basket.
    If no fruit of that type is left on the tree, nothing happens. You should roll the dice once more.
  • The raven. Oh dear! The raven approaches the orchard.
    The raven will be moved one path card forward.
  • The fruit basket.
    Click any fruit and it will be placed in the basket.

Apples = 4
Cherries = 4
Pears = 4
Plums = 4
